How to use

  • Channel is a concept of a call circuit in RemoteMonster SDK
  • You can create a channel or join a channel.
  • "Channels" shows the list of channels you can join. The channel you create from this page doesn't show on the list.
  • To create a channel, please enter a channel name and click the "CONNECT" button.
  • Open the same link in another tab or a browser. To join the channel you created, click on the channel name from the channel list.
  • Please use a unique channel name.
